Content Strategy Brief — Concept

Wellness brand for the non-wellness person

Most wellness content talks to people who are already well. This brief targets the gap: people who are struggling, sceptical of wellness culture, and just trying to get through the week.

The strategy centres on radical honesty, low-barrier entry content, and a community-first approach to building trust before asking for anything in return.

Honest storytelling Community-first Low-barrier entry Anti-toxic-positivity

Caption Framework — Concept

The "tomorrow is a new day" format

A repeatable Instagram caption structure built on three beats: acknowledge the hard thing, reframe it without dismissing it, close with one small actionable thought. No inspiration-porn. Just honest, warm, useful.
